About Us

Welcome to the Bowdoin Wine Club! We are a group of wine enthusiasts at Bowdoin College who are passionate about exploring and learning about different wines from around the world. Whether you are a seasoned wine connoisseur or just starting to discover your love for wine, you are welcome to join our club.

Our club meets regularly to taste and discuss a variety of wines from different regions. We welcome anyone who is interested in learning more about wine and exploring different varieties to join our club. Don't tell administration ;)
